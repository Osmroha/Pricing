Information

Moonbin & Sanha is the first official sub-unit of South Korean boy band Astro. Formed by Fantagio in 2020, the group is composed of two Astro members: Moon Bin & Yoon San-ha. Their debut extended play, 1stEP In-Out was released on September 14, 2020 with the title track "Bad Idea". And they came back with 2ndEP Refuge released on March 15, 2022 with the title track "Who". This year they came back with 3rdEP Insence released on January 4,2023 with the title track "Madness".

Information

Astro is a South Korean boy band formed by Fantagio that debuted in 2016. The group is composed of five members: MJ, Jinjin, Cha Eun-woo, Moon Bin, and Yoon San-ha. Originally a six-piece group, Rocky departed the group on February 28,2023.
